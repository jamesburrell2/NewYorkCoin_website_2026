Born in New York

Our Story

The Blockchain Built for the People, by the People

NewYorkCoin launched March 6, 2014 — a Litecoin fork designed from day one to be lightning-fast and completely fee-free. No ICO. No pre-mine. Pure, open-source, community-driven cryptocurrency.

In 2017, a volunteer development team committed to realizing NYC's full potential as a global, permission-less, zero-fee payment solution — establishing the New York Coin Center to lead the effort. Today, that mission continues with a full modernization of the codebase and wallet ecosystem.

Core Properties

Built Different. By Design.

Three properties that set NewYorkCoin apart from every other cryptocurrency.

Instant Confirmation

Transactions confirm in 30 seconds — more than 20× faster than Bitcoin and 5× faster than Litecoin. Accept NYC at the register without waiting.

Zero Transaction Fees

Send and receive NYC anywhere in the world for free — forever. No mining fees, no congestion surcharges. The only Layer 1 blockchain with true zero-cost transfers.

Secure & Anonymous

Scrypt proof-of-work consensus. Fully decentralized — no central authority, no admin keys. Your transactions are private. Your coins are yours.

Roadmap

NYC is Getting a Major Upgrade

The community is actively building the next generation of NewYorkCoin — bringing it to feature parity with the latest Litecoin codebase and beyond.

In Development

MimbleWimble Extension Blocks (MWEB)

Porting Litecoin's flagship privacy upgrade to NewYorkCoin. MWEB introduces Confidential Transactions — hiding sender, receiver, and amounts from the public ledger while remaining fully verifiable on-chain. True financial privacy, finally available on NYC.

01
In Development

Modern BIP Compliance

Full modernization of the NYC codebase to support the latest Bitcoin Improvement Proposals — including SegWit, updated scripting capabilities, and hardened network security upgrades. NYC will be fully compatible with the modern Bitcoin and Litecoin ecosystem.

02
In Development

Coinomi-Style Multi-Coin Wallet

A full-featured, non-custodial mobile and desktop wallet — built with NYC as the centerpiece, alongside support for major cryptocurrencies. Clean UX, hardware wallet support, and built-in exchange. Your keys. Your coins. One app.

03
Ongoing

Merchant Payment Infrastructure

NYC's zero-fee, 30-second confirmation model makes it ideal for retail. We're building merchant tooling — point-of-sale integrations, payment processor APIs, and e-commerce plugins — to make accepting NYC effortless for businesses worldwide.
